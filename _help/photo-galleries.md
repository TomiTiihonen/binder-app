Photo Gallery entries are for sets of photos that belong together — a dinner party, a hike, a day out.

## Creating a gallery

1. Tap **+** and choose **Photo Gallery**.
2. Pick a cover photo — this represents the gallery in the journal list.
3. Add additional photos to the gallery grid.
4. Add a caption.

On macOS, you can drag photos straight from Finder onto the journal list to create a gallery in one step. See [Drag and drop](/help/drag-and-drop/).

## The viewer

Tap any photo to open the full-screen viewer. Swipe to move between photos.

## Gallery photos on other entry types

Several entry types also support gallery photos — Events, Recommendations, Location Pins, and Lists. Use a dedicated Photo Gallery entry when the photos themselves *are* the point; use gallery photos on another entry type when they support something else.
