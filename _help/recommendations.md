Recommendations are your personal review archive. Rate a restaurant, a book, a film, a coffee shop — anything worth remembering for next time or worth telling a friend about.

## Creating a recommendation

1. Tap **+** and choose **Recommendation**.
2. Add a name and category (e.g. *Restaurant*, *Book*, *Film*).
3. Tap the stars to rate it.
4. Optionally add a location, website, photos, and notes.

## Half-star precision

Star ratings support half stars. On macOS, click and drag across the stars to set any value from half a star to five.

## Fields

- **Name** — what you're rating
- **Category** — restaurant, book, album, gadget, anything
- **Rating** — 0 to 5 stars, half-star precision
- **Location** — for places
- **Website** — link to a book, venue, article
- **Cover and gallery photos**
- **Notes** — why you liked it, what to order, who to recommend it to

## Reminders

Set a reminder to revisit a recommendation — e.g. "suggest this restaurant to Alex next week".
