Almost every entry type in Binder can have a reminder. Attach one to a note, a link, a recommendation — anything you want to come back to — and Binder sends a notification at the time you choose.

## Adding a reminder

1. Open or create the entry.
2. Find the **Reminder** field in the edit form.
3. Pick a date and time.

The entry will show a bell badge to indicate a reminder is set.

## Which entry types support reminders

Reminders are supported on every entry type *except* Daily Mood and Photo Gallery:

- Notes, Voice Memos, Lists, Links, Files
- Events, Recommendations, Location Pins
- Collections

## Notifications

When a reminder fires, Binder posts a local notification. Tap it to jump straight to the entry.

Make sure notification permissions are enabled for Binder in system Settings — otherwise reminders will fire silently.

## Clearing a reminder

Open the entry, edit the reminder field, and remove the date.
