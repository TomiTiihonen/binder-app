Lists are checklists — each row is an item you can tick off. Use them for to-dos, shopping, packing, reading lists, or any set of things where order and completion matter.

## Creating a list

1. Tap **+** and choose **List**.
2. Give the list a title and optional category (e.g. *Groceries*, *Packing*).
3. Add items one at a time.

## Ticking items off

Tap the checkbox next to any item to mark it done. Done items stay visible so you can see your progress.

## Reordering and editing

Drag items to reorder. Tap an item to edit its text.

## Photos

Lists support gallery photos — attach images to illustrate items, useful for packing lists or shopping lists with product references.

## Reminders

Attach a reminder to the list as a whole (e.g. "Review packing list tomorrow"). See [Reminders](/help/reminders/).
