Location pins are places worth remembering — a bench with a great view, a shop you want to come back to, the exact spot where something happened.

## Creating a pin

1. Tap **+** and choose **Location Pin**.
2. Give the pin a title.
3. Pick the location from the map or search for an address.
4. Optionally add photos, notes, and a reminder.

## Map thumbnail

The detail view shows a map thumbnail centred on the pin. Tap it to open the full map or hand off to the system Maps app for directions.

## Place name vs. address

Binder prefers a point-of-interest name (like "Kyoto Imperial Palace") over a street address when reverse-geocoding a dropped pin, so your pins read naturally instead of as coordinates.

## Fields

- **Title**
- **Location** — latitude, longitude, and place name
- **Notes**
- **Cover and gallery photos**
- **Reminder**

## When to use a pin vs. a recommendation

- **Pin**: a place you want to remember for yourself
- **Recommendation**: a place (or thing) you want to *rate* and potentially share
