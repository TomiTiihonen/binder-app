Several entry types can be pinned to a place — Events, Recommendations, and Location Pins. The location shows up as a map thumbnail in the detail view and is searchable by name.

## Picking a location

In the entry's edit form, tap the **Location** field:

- Search by address, place name, or business
- Drop a pin directly on the map
- Use your current location

Once set, the entry shows a map thumbnail with the pin.

## Place names vs. addresses

When you drop a pin, Binder prefers a point-of-interest name (a park, museum, venue) over a street address for the place name field. This keeps entries readable — "Kyoto Imperial Palace" rather than a long address.

## Opening in Maps

Tap the map thumbnail to open the system Maps app for directions or to see the place in a bigger context.

## Which entry types support locations

- **Events** — where something happened
- **Recommendations** — where a place or thing is
- **Location Pins** — the place *is* the entry
