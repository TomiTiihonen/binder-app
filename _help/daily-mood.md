Daily mood entries are the fastest entry in Binder — a single tap to log how you're feeling, with an optional note.

## Logging a mood

1. Tap **+** and choose **Daily Mood**.
2. Pick a mood.
3. Optionally jot a quick note about why.

That's it. No reminders, no photos — just a check-in.

## Why lightweight matters

The friction of logging shapes whether you do it at all. Daily mood is intentionally stripped down so the cost of pressing "record" is near zero.

## Reviewing moods over time

Search for "moods this month" or scroll back through your journal to see patterns. Because every entry is timestamped, you can review your history any time.
